Strawberry Salad

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 15 Min

This salad combines juicy strawberries, savory feta cheese, toasted walnuts and fresh mint leaves into one delicious bowl. Serve right away on a hot summer day for a cooling treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw spinach
  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves

Directions

  1. Roughly chop the walnuts and toast in a small pan over medium heat until golden brown.
  2. Place the spinach in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Slice the strawberries and add to the bowl with the spinach.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the olive oil, vinegar, honey and salt.
  5. Pour the mixture into the large mixing bowl. Lightly toss to coat.
  6. Divide the salad into bowls and sprinkle feta, mint, and toasted walnuts on top.

Classic Basil Pesto

Prep Time: 5 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 5 Min

This gorgeous pesto is full of color and flavor! Serve on your favorite pasta for a delicious, healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 8-12 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 cup packed basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Directions

  1. In a small pan, toast the pine nuts over medium heat until golden.
  2. In a food processor, pulse garlic, toasted pine nuts and basil together.
  3. Add parmesan cheese, lemon juice and salt. Pulse again.
  4. Add olive oil and pulse until smooth. If the mixture is too thick, add more oil or hot water to thin.

Gluten-Free Sugar Cookies

Prep Time: 10 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 20 Min

These buttery cookies have a tender texture and crispy exterior that you're sure to love!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups gluten-free flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1/2 cup softened, unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Add flour, salt, baking powder and xanthan gum to a bowl and stir.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and sugar together.
  4. Add egg and vanilla extract to the butter and sugar mixture.
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients in a bowl. Stir until fully combined.
  6. Drop teaspoon-sized dough onto the parchment paper. Keep dough 1 to 2 inches apart. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
